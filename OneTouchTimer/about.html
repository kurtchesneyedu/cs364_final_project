About Us

Kurt Chesney

My name is Kurt, and I grew up in Aliso Viejo, CA. Growing up in California, I never realized how much I appreciated the beach until I moved away, and no longer had access to it in Colorado. I was always outdoors as a kid, and I still enjoy climbing, fishing, camping, off-roading, anything to get me outside. Compsci was a clear passion for me, and pulled me toward the Academy in middle school. Later, football gave me a really good shot to guarantee admission into USAFA, and playing at the collegiate level is a dream come true.

At USAFA, I'm Bull Six's SEL and I lead the position group on the football team. Football and school takes up most of my time, but I still find it super fulfilling to work with squad as much as I can. If I'm not at school, you can find me outside, off-roading my truck to a fly fishing spot and camping or snowboarding in the winter. On the football team, I'm the starting long snapper and have been since freshman year. I played mike backer in high school but switched to only special teams in college and haven't looked back since.

In the future, I want to commission as a 17S with a focus on red teaming. Hoping to soak up as much as I can in college to make that dream a reality. Not sure about staying in for 5 or leaving, but I know I want to do private sector work after the military. One of my best friends attends the University of Washington studying compsci and working as the head of their rocketry club. We have plans to do pentesting and redteam contracting in the future.
